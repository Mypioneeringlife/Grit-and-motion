Samsung Steps Sync Test

Samsung Health can stay as your step counter. The native Android app reads steps through Health Connect once you set up sharing and grant permission. Manual step entry stays active the whole time.
  1. 1Open Samsung Health and confirm it is counting your steps.
  2. 2Connect Samsung Health to Health Connect in Samsung Health settings.
  3. 3Allow step data sharing from Samsung Health into Health Connect.
  4. 4Return to Grit & Motion, tap Connect Health Connect, then tap Sync today’s steps.
